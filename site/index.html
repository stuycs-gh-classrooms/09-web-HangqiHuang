A Brief Introduction to G.E.M.

Many people have never heard of G.E.M., and that's absolutely intolerable. She is such a good singer and it is a shame that one shouldn't know of her. For this reason, this website is created to inform the uninformed of who is G.E.M. and why you should listen to her.

This website includes a brief description of how G.E.M. became who is, some of her most famous songs, and why she is such a good singer and my attempt to persuade you to listen to her.
