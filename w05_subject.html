Background

G.E.M. is a music artist who grew up in Hongkong, China. When she was 15, she won the champion in a singing competition called "Spice It Up" and caught the attention of Chang Tan, who offered her a recording deal with Hummingbird Music. With the help of Hummingbird Music, she released many successful albums and became very successful. However, in 2019, she parted ways with Hummingbird Music, claiming that the company unfairly treated her. She became an independent singer and continued releasing songs. She is considered one of the most famous and successful Chinese singer. See Wikipedia for more information.

Why She is My Favorite Artist

G.E.M. is a very talented artist and a lot of her songs are about things she personally notices about the world. In this way, her songs resonate with me and sometimes really expresses how I feel. This makes me feel like having a personal connection with her. She's like an old friend I never met. My favorite song by G.E.M. is Double Me.
